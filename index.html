ABOUT AMICORUM

AMICORUM (Latin word close to the meaning of Gathering of Friends) will be a crowdsourced blockchain-based peer-peer ecosystem for ticket resale of music festivals and concerts around the globe. Buyers will be able to buy tickets with the AMI token (the new cryptocurrency based on Ethereum blockchain) from the Sellers at “0” transaction costs.
AMICORUM.LIVE will be an innovative new blockchain trading platform that will disrupt the music festival/concert ticket ecosystem and decentralize the ticket re-sale markets. This ecosystem will provide a possibility for everyone to buy tickets without being affected by transaction costs and processing fees.
